# Changelog 

## v1.0.2
- Added an option to make the motor's rotation show yellow hit cooldown time 
- Split pitch, volume, and opacity options for yellow hit cooldown steam by yellow hit count 
- Added yellow hit cooldown steam particle count options by yellow hit count 
- Steam volume and particle count now increases with yellow hit count by default 
- Renamed "Cooldown Particle Color" and "Cooldown Particle Opacity" to "Long Cooldown Particle Color" and "Long Cooldown Particle Opacity"
- Split "Disable on long cooldown" into "Disable audio on long cooldown" and "Disable particles on long cooldown"
- Added headers to the config 

## v1.0.1 
- Fixed a bug where the steam would not reenable itself when swapping to a hammer that had steam enabled the last time it was held 
- Added an option to have both the yellow timer steam and long cooldown steam enabled at once 

## v1.0.0 
- Initial release
